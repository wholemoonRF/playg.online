Game Overview

Monster Survivors is a thrilling survival action game where players battle endless waves of mysterious creatures. Collect experience gems to unlock powerful upgrades, enhance your abilities, and survive as long as possible in this ever-increasing challenge.

How to Play

  • Move with WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Auto-attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose powerful upgrades each level
  • Survive as long as possible!

Features

  • Endless survival gameplay
  • 20+ unique power-ups
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Leaderboard integration
  • Mobile-friendly controls
